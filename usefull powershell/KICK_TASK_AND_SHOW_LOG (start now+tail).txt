9) KICK_TASK_AND_SHOW_LOG (start now + tail)

Starts the logged-in task immediately and shows the latest log tail.

# --- 9_KICK_TASK_AND_SHOW_LOG ---
$task="OPRT Hourly Run (Logged-In)"; $logs="C:\OPRT\logs\run_*.log"
Start-ScheduledTask -TaskName $task
Start-Sleep 5
Get-ChildItem $logs | Sort-Object LastWriteTime -Desc | Select-Object -First 1 | Get-Content -Tail 80
